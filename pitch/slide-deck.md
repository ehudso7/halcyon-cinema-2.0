## Slide 5: Cinema Mode

### From Prose to Production

**Input:** Scene from your manuscript
```
Elena stood in the doorway, her hand trembling
on the frame. Marcus turned, and for a moment
neither spoke. The weight of three years of
silence hung between them.
```

**Output:** Shot Sequence
| # | Type | Description |
|---|------|-------------|
| 1 | Wide | Doorway frame, Elena silhouetted |
| 2 | Close-up | Elena's hand trembling on frame |
| 3 | Medium | Marcus turning, recognition |
| 4 | Two-shot | Both figures, space between them |

**+ Visual prompts for each shot**
