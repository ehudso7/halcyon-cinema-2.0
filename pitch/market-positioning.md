### Primary: Serious Fiction Writers
- **Size**: 500K+ active in US alone
- **Pain**: Continuity errors, no path to adaptation
- **Spend**: $10-50/mo on tools
- **Why Us**: Canon Vault + optional Cinema

### Secondary: Screenwriters
- **Size**: 100K+ WGA and aspiring
- **Pain**: Visualization for pitches
- **Spend**: $20-100/mo on tools
- **Why Us**: Direct shot generation from scenes

### Tertiary: Indie Filmmakers
- **Size**: 200K+ active globally
- **Pain**: Pre-production costs
- **Spend**: $50-500/mo on pre-pro tools
- **Why Us**: Affordable storyboarding from script
